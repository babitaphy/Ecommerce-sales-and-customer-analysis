# Ecommerce-sales-and-customer-analysis
Business Insights and Recommendations
Electronics category dominates revenue, contributing the majority of sales.
Laptops are the highest revenue-generating product.
A few customers contribute significantly to total revenue (high-value customers).
Sales trend shows consistent demand across the month.
Recommendations:
Focus marketing on high-value products like laptops.
Retain high-value customers with loyalty programs.
Improve performance of low-selling categories like clothing.
